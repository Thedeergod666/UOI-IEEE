About IEEE Student Branch

The IEEE Student Branch at University of Leicester is a vibrant community of engineering and technology enthusiasts dedicated to professional development, networking, and technological innovation.

Our History

Founded in 2024, our student branch has grown from a small group of passionate students to a thriving community of over 200 members. We've hosted numerous successful events and competitions.

Our Activities

From technical workshops and hackathons to industry visits and networking events, we offer diverse opportunities for skill development and professional growth.

Our Achievements

Recognized as the "Best New Student Branch 2024" and winners of multiple regional competitions, our members consistently demonstrate excellence in engineering and technology.

Read more

Our Mission

To foster an inclusive community where students can explore the frontiers of technology, develop professional skills, and build meaningful connections that will shape their future careers in engineering and related fields.

We believe in the power of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact on society.
